Providing Quality from Quality Practices

Breeding Rams

Our rams are genetically selected to provide well-muscled, fast-growing, and healthy offspring that create large litters while breeding out of season

Breeding Ewes

From lambs to yearlings and beyond, we supply breeding stock that complement our breeding rams by provide exceptional offspring at all times of the year

Market Lambs

Ehrhardt Farm's market lambs are always the most well-muscled, fast-growing, and healthy in their class

Research Animals

Having more than 60 years of academic animal science research experience and a decade of experience supplying animals to research firms, we have a deep understanding of what scientific research requires and how to provide it

High-Quality Pastures
Our sheep graze on the highest-quality mixed-legume-grass pastures, providing enough energy, protein, and micronutrients for pregnant ewes carrying twins and triplets
Aseasonal Breeding Stock
Ehrhardt Farm breeding stock are carefully selected to breed year-round and provide highly-desirable maternal traits
Pasture Lambing
Our ewes lamb on pasture both in the spring and fall to provide lambs year-round
Winter Grazing
We graze our ewes into the winter on neary farmers' cover crops, providing enough nutrition for late pregnant ewes carrying twins and triplets
Accelerated Lambing
Lambing three times a year, we raise our animals inside and outside, rain or shine, in sun or snow, to maximizes health, comfort, and safety
Lamb Finishing
Depending on the season, our lambs are finished on a diet of concentrates and high-energy forages
